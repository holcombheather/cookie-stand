Our Story

Our founder, Pat, combined his love for sweets with his passion for healthy ecosystems to create a coffee-time confection that is not only delicious but also environmentally friendly. Made in the shape of a salmon, our cookies are a fun and unique way to indulge in a sweet treat while being mindful of the planet.

At Pat's Salmon Cookies, we take sustainability seriously. That's why we use only the highest quality, responsibly sourced ingredients in every cookie we make. Our commitment to eco-friendliness extends beyond our ingredients, as we use recyclable and biodegradable packaging for our products.
We believe that delicious treats can be made in a way that supports healthy ecosystems, and we're proud to be leading the way in the cookie industry.

So come join us and indulge in a treat that's as good for the planet as it is for your taste buds. Try Pat's Salmon Cookies today!
